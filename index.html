Worlds to Explore, Adventures to Live

Bethesda Game Studios is a renowned developer creating immersive game worlds since 1986. We're the minds behind legendary franchises like The Elder Scrolls and Fallout, known for their expansive open worlds, rich stories, and endless player freedom.

Our Legacy of Award-Winning Games

    A sprawling fantasy RPG series where you carve your own path through mythical lands, fulfilling epic quests and shaping the destiny of empires.

  • Fallout

    A post-apocalyptic RPG saga set in a world ravaged by nuclear war. Explore the wasteland, scavenge for resources, and choose your role in rebuilding civilization.

  • Starfield (Upcoming)

    Embark on a next-generation role-playing adventure in the vast expanse of space. Explore a starfield of unprecedented scale, forge alliances, and unravel cosmic mysteries.
